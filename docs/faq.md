# MeshSats FAQ

## Is MeshSats a new Bitcoin token?
**No.** MeshSats uses Bitcoin and the Lightning Network for settlement. There are no proprietary tokens.

## How do you prevent double-spending offline?
We manage risk through signed intents, transaction caps, and pre-funded agent liquidity. See our [Risk and Trust Model](risk_and_trust_model.md) for a full, honest explanation.

## Does it require a specialty hardware?
No. While we envision specialized low-cost POS devices, the MeshSats protocol can run on standard smartphones via QR codes and Bluetooth.

## Who is this for?
MeshSats is infrastructure for developers building tools for African market vendors, waste collectors, agent networks, and community payout programs.

## Is it fully trustless?
Offline payments on Bitcoin have inherent trust trade-offs. MeshSats identifies the most practical points of trust (e.g., local agents, pre-funded hubs) to enable commerce in zero-connectivity areas while maintaining a cryptographic audit trail.
